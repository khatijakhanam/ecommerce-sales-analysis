Business Insights & Recommendations
Electronics → Loyalty Gap
Electronics generates 40% of revenue but only 18% repeat rate. Introduce a post-purchase loyalty program (points, extended warranty) to improve retention by an estimated 8–12%.
November Peak → Capitalize Early
November is the highest revenue month (2.4× monthly avg). Launch pre-campaign ads in October to capture early holiday shoppers and extend deal windows into December.
At-Risk Segment → Win-Back Campaign
43% of customers are At-Risk/New. A targeted email campaign with a 15% discount for customers inactive 60+ days can recover 8–12% churn and lift repeat rate significantly.
West Region → Untapped Market
West region contributes only 11% of revenue despite 22% population coverage. Increase localized ad spend and offer region-specific bundles to unlock latent demand.
